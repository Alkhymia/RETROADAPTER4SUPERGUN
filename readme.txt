RETROADAPTER4SUPERGUN aka The Kraken

50 x 50 mm
1.2 to 1.6 (recommended) mm Thickness
HASL

Uses the concept of Paul Quereshi's RetroAdapter, but with CommonGround-Output instead of USB.
Lets you connect a wide range of controllers to a supergun incl SNES, MD, SAT, PSX, N64 etc.
